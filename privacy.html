Privacy Policy

This calculator runs entirely in your web browser. No form entries or results are transmitted to any server.

We do not collect or store personal information. A short history of your recent calculations is kept only in memory for the current session and is cleared when you reload or close the page.

Styles and scripts are loaded from the jsDelivr CDN. That provider may log standard web request information such as your IP address.

If you have any questions about these practices, please contact the project maintainer.
